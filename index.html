Mission

b9 is founded in 2021. Our mission is to make a resilient world. We believe all the solutions are invented, we only need to implement them. b9 is a design office. We create buildings, urban plans and strategic interventions. b9 designs within the ecosystem. Therefor all actors should be considered and every design should be benign to our ecosphere.
